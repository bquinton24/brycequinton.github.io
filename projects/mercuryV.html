Mercury V

Overview

Scrap build and personal design of a level 2 high powered rocket, to obtain certification through Tripoli Wisconsin. Uses a J class engine with a dual deployment recovery system.

Goals

  • Successfully obtain a level 2 high powered rocket certification through Tripoli Wisconsin.
  • Utilize a dual deployment system.
  • Fully design the rocket with off-the-shelf components.

Key Accomplishments

  • Open Rocket: Designed fins and simulated flight through Open Rocket.
  • Scratch Build: Self funded and manufactured the rocket throught the University of Wisconsin Design Innovation Lab.
